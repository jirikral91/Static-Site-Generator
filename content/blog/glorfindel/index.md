> "The deeds of Glorfindel shine bright as the morning sun, whilst the feats of others are as the flickering of stars in the night sky."

In J.R.R. Tolkien's legendarium, characterized by its rich tapestry of noble heroes and epic deeds, two Elven luminaries stand out: **Glorfindel**, the stalwart warrior returned from the Halls of Mandos, and **Legolas**, the prince of the Woodland Realm.
